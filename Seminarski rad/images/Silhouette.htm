Kearney woman found safe

Posted by Matt Griffith | Aug 23, 2017 | Local News, News

UPDATE: 5:30 p.m. – The missing individual has been found and is safe.

KEARNEY, MO – The Clay County Sheriff’s Office is asking for public assistance in locating a woman who may have been seeking medical attention.

According to a press release, Whitney Brooke Duffy, 29, of Kearney, Missouri was reported missing Tuesday just after 11:00 p.m. She was last seen in the Kearney area. Duffy is described as 5 feet 2 inches, 115 pounds with brown hair and blue eyes. She was last seen wearing a pink top, black shirt, black yoga pants, and black and pink shoes.

Anyone with information is asked to call the Clay County Sheriff’s Office Investigations Unit at 816-407-3729.
